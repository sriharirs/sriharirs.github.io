The last step of the “Spot The Pattern” task is to upload the data. After you press the right arrow key, a pop-up message will appear. This message will be similar to the image below. PLEASE WAIT for this message to appear and then press OK.

After that, please press CONTINUE on top of the screen to upload the data. 

Note: Pressing CONTINUE before pressing OK on the pop-up would result in loss of data (Thank you again for your patience!).
